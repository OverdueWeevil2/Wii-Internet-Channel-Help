8      
  Parental Controls
       
 
 
 

There are two ways to restrict content viewable through the Internet Channel:
● Enabling the Parental Controls settings
Parental Controls is one of the features included in Wii. This feature allows parents or legal guardians of children to limit access to certain content, such as the use of certain software titles and the purchase of software via the Wii Shop Channel. The Internet Channel allows the following functions to be restricted:
• The use of the Internet Channel.

• When using the browser itself:
- The PROXY SETTINGS option on the Settings Screen.

Parental Controls settings can be accessed from the Wii Menu by selecting Wii Settings, then Parental Controls. Refer to the Wii Settings and Data Management section of the Wii Operations Manual – Channels and Settings for details.
● Using the Filtering Service
A fee-based filtering service to prevent potentially harmful sites being viewed can also be used. For further details on the service, go to the Favorites Screen and select ASTARO.
IMPORTANT POINTS REGARDING THE FILTERING SERVICE:
• You must adjust the proxy settings to enable content filtering on the Internet Channel. To do so, go to the Settings Screen from the Start Page, and select the PROXY SETTINGS option.
• If you need to set up a proxy to enable your Wii console to connect to the Internet, you can do so in Wii Settings. However, by doing so, any proxy settings made through the Internet Channel, as well as the content filtering service, will become inactive.
