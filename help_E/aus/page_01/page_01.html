In addition, Nintendo is in no way affiliated with the the content available through e-mail, message boards, blogs or other general online content. Please use the Internet Channel responsibly. Use of the Internet Channel is subject to the Wii Network Service Agreement, the Wii Network Service Code of Conduct and the Wii Network Service Privacy Policy.

When using the Internet Channel to enter text into a website, please keep the following in mind:

1. Avoid writing messages that other people could find disturbing or offensive.

2. Do not give out personal information (such as addresses, phone numbers or e-mail addresses) for yourself or others when unnecessary.

Websites on the Internet may contain information which viewers may find offensive, or which is inaccurate, dangerous, illegal, etc. Please be very careful of such information. We strongly recommend that a parent or legal guardian monitors any websites to be viewed by a young child. For information on parental control functionalities please refer to “8. Parental Controls”.

Websites, including the pictures and wording displayed therein, are protected by copyright law. Unauthorised reproduction, distribution, alteration and reprinting of any of this material is not allowed. For more information please read the Wii Network Service Code of Conduct.

When using the Search function from the Internet Channel toolbar, your search query will be sent from Nintendo's servers to your designated search engine. Nintendo may collect anonymous usage statistics in connection with the search query, but does not collect personal information.
