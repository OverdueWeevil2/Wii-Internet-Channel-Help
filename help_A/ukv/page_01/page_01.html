About the Internet Channel

When using the Internet Channel, please keep the following in mind:

Nintendo takes no responsibility for loss or damage that occurs as a result of connecting to the Internet through the Internet Channel or for damage that occurs as a result of failing to connect. In addition, Nintendo is in no way affiliated with the content available through e-mail, message boards, blogs or other general online content. Please use the Internet Channel responsibly. Use of the Internet Channel is subject to the Wii Network Service End User License Agreement, the Wii Network Service Code of Conduct and the Wii Network Service Privacy Policy.

When using the Internet Channel to enter text into a Website, please keep the following in mind:

1. Avoid writing messages that other people could find disturbing or offensive.

2. Do not give out personal information (such as addresses, phone numbers or e-mail addresses) for yourself or others when unnecessary.

There are Websites on the Internet that display disturbing, inaccurate, dangerous, and illegal information. Please bear this in mind when browsing the Internet. Nintendo asks that guardians pay special attention to young children when they are viewing Web content.

Websites, including the pictures and wording displayed therein, are protected by copyright law. Unauthorized reproduction, distribution, alteration and reprinting of any of this material is not allowed.

When using the Search function from the Internet Channel's toolbar, your search query will be sent from Nintendo's servers to your designated search engine so Nintendo will have access to anonymous usage statistics in connection with the search query.
