8      
  Restricting Use
       
 
 
 
There are two methods for restricting use of the Internet Channel.
 Use Parental Controls
Parental Controls are a feature of the Wii console. Parents and guardians can restrict certain features using this tool. Access to the Internet Channel can be completely restricted by setting the corresponding Parental Control.
To turn on Parental Controls, please visit the Parental Control Settings screen in Wii Options. For further details, see the “Parental Controls” section of in the Wii Operations Manual.
 Use of a Filtering Service
You can use a third party filtering service (sold separately) that restricts access to potentially offensive web pages. Depending on what third party service you select, you can enter the proxy information in the "Proxy Settings" field in this software's "Settings" option.
