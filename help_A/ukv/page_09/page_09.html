  Glossary
       
 
 
 
Address (URL)
An address indicates a Website's location on the Internet. Addresses are also known as URLs.

Browser
A browser is a piece of software that allows you to view Websites on the Internet. The Internet Channel uses a full Internet browser developed by Opera Software.

Search
To search on the Internet means to filter through all publicly available information on the Web to find information that matches your keyword. On the Internet Channel, you can run the search function using either Google or Yahoo! as your preferred search engine.

Proxy
A server that allows users to make an indirect connection to the Internet by receiving the information requested by the user first before relaying it to the user's machine.

Cookie
A cookie is a piece of data that holds information about a Website you have visited. Cookies are transmitted from the Websites you visit and are stored on your Wii. With this data, your Wii can track when and how often you have accessed a given Website, although we will not link your personal information with these cookies without first notifying you.
